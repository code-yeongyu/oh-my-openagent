# Why the correction evidence is enough

- Disablement is proved at planner level with and without explicit model, and at the advertised-roster level.
- Execution pinning is proved after config overlay and by the real in-process child path.
- Read-only behavior is enforced by an unambiguous schema, direct executable invocation without a shell, positive command/flag allowlists, unit mutation cases, and live filesystem absence checks.
- Bundle freshness is content-based across Bun's transitive graph; the regression suite names the primary senpi-task broker input and rejects stale source/body markers irrespective of mtimes.
- Drift guards are independent literals: full fallback tables and a complete expected analyzer check object.
- The final typecheck, package, adapter, generated-bundle, documentation, and real-harness gates all pass.
